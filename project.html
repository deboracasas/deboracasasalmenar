UX UI design

Crosscorner

The problem.

People who perform Crossfit at competitive level, they experience difficulties when it comes to recording their qualifying videos, because they have to go through more than 4 platforms to attend a competition.

The competitions of Crossfit are disorganized in several platforms reason why to the user it is to him difficult to find them.

The users who perfom this kind of sport, they like to be on top of all events and news around crossfit world.

The research.

The 80% of the people surveyed affirm that the process by which they have to pass to be finally registered in the competition, is tedious.

The 95% of survey respondents have experienced problems when they have to record their qualifying videos to be registered in the competition.

The solution.

All users who practice Crossfit use the mobile device, both to record their WODs and to look for competitions and be informed. That is why the solution to the problem is: create a platform where there is a list of all the competitions worldwide that you can filter by name or by country and it has a direct access to record the qualifier imposed by the organizations of the competitions. Also, as a result of the research, I realized that users wanted to be informed about the events and news and to be able to follow a planning that Crossfit offers to users of any level.

Main features:

  • Access and direct registration to all competitions worldwide.
  • Recording videos through the application itself.
  • Daily news from the Crossfit world.
  • To be conected with others athletes anywhere in the world.
  • Save and share your own qualifiers.
