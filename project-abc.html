UX UI design

ABC Groceries

The problem.

ABC Company’s vision is to design a user experience that goes above and beyond the e-commerce marketplace models that Amazon and other competitors (like Walmart) are offering.

They want to add value for their customers by finding a way to leverage their successful premium store model and replicate it into a differentiating digital experience.

The research.

75% of the people surveyed use a computer to make their purchases online, of which 56% distrust the delivery service and even more in online supermarkets and 40% distrust of fresh products.

67% like to go shopping at the physical supermarket because they buy deli and butcher products with cut to suit each client.

The solution.

For each of the problems we saw reflected in the surveys and interviews, we give these types of solutions:

  • Offer personalized cuts of meat and the type of packaging.
  • The consumer will be able to track the detailed order, where he will be able to know exactly the location and the time it will take.
  • The user has shared lists with members of his family or friends, which will help him to remember the products that are forgotten based on his shopping habits.
