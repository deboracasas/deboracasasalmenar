UX UI design

TravelMates

The problem.

People who want to travel with friends, have problems to match the dates of the trip.

Travelers want to find the best prices for flights and hotels to spend in the least money possible.

People who travel frequently with friends, would like to have a shared budget to distribute group expenses.

On the other hand, I also wanted to know the problems that people who were traveling alone had, and I found such as: some people do not have who to visit a city and for that reason they do not travel. Also because his friends have already visited that city and don´t want to repeat the destination again and that's why they don´t travel alone.

The research.

72% of people surveyed say they find it difficult to organize a trip with friends.

65% The first thing they considered when organizing is the dates.

For 82%, the most difficult factor when organizing a trip in this order are: dates, price, destination and itinerary.

78% say that the most complicated to organize are trips to the foreign.

In personal interviews, people said they use whatsapp or doddle to organize trips, but they still don´t agree.

The solution.

Social platform that synchronizes the dates of the personal calendar with each person you want to travel with and offers you the best days, the best destination for those dates, prices of flights and hotels based on the group budget that has previously been adjusted in each of the profiles individual users.

On the other hand, for those people who haven´t anyone to travel with, there is the possibility of connecting with other people who also travel alone, but want to meet new people along the way. Each person can publish the trip they want to make, giving information such as the itinerary and the days that will happen in this place. Other people can join the trip and contact them.
